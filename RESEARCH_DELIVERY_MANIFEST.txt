KEY FINDINGS AT A GLANCE
================================================================================

UNIVERSAL GAPS (What NO competitor does):
❌ Intelligent CSV schema inference (all template-based)
❌ EDI 204/990 support (enterprise bottleneck)
❌ PDF manifest parsing (requires manual entry)
❌ ML-based anomaly detection (no proactive flagging)
❌ Postal authority validation (GNAF, PAF, USPS)
❌ Automated duplicate detection
❌ Real-time data quality monitoring
❌ Webhook-based error recovery

COMPETITIVE POSITIONING:

Locate2u:
  Strengths: Shopify integration, Australia-focused, transparent pricing
  Weaknesses: Template-based CSV, address validation limited to Google
  Opportunity: GNAF integration, intelligent field mapping

Onfleet:
  Strengths: Batch API, address warning system
  Weaknesses: Manual column mapping, strict address format rules
  Opportunity: Automatic schema inference, anomaly detection

OptimoRoute:
  Strengths: Geocoding confidence controls, fallback lat/long
  Weaknesses: No intelligent parsing, validation too late
  Opportunity: Schema inference, postal authority validation

Shippit:
  Strengths: 100+ carrier integrations, manifest PDF generation
  Weaknesses: No CSV import visible, order-by-order only
  Opportunity: Batch manifest processing, data quality

Routific:
  Strengths: Drag-drop import, smart region deduction
  Weaknesses: No field inference, no EDI, no duplicate detection
  Opportunity: Intelligent field mapping, anomaly detection

THE OPPORTUNITY:

Market: $100M+ TAM in last-mile delivery data normalization
Timeline: 12 months to MVP, 18-24 months to competitive parity
Team: 7-9 engineers, $500k-700k Year 1 investment
Moat: ML models + data quality insights + postal authority integration
Stickiness: Win at onboarding = 3-5 year customer lock-in

"10x Better" vs. Competitors:
- CSV onboarding: <10 min (vs 60 min manual mapping)
- Address match rate: 97%+ AU (vs 85-90% baseline)
- Anomaly detection: 80%+ recall (competitors: 0%)
- EDI support: Native 204/990 (competitors: none)
