## Competitive Positioning

### Best For:

**Locate2u**
- Transparent, per-seat pricing
- Global operations (AU, US, UK, CA, NZ, IN)
- Mid-market with simple needs
- Fast time-to-value

**Onfleet**
- Mature, well-funded platform
- Enterprise with complex requirements
- Multiple feature integrations
- Premium positioning

**OptimoRoute**
- Cost-conscious small-medium operations
- Per-driver billing predictability
- Field service + delivery hybrid
- 30-day free trial, no contract

**Routific**
- Fast route optimization priority
- Usage-based billing preference
- 179 global ML models
- High-volume, time-sensitive operations

### Worst For:

**Locate2u**
- Large enterprises (better alternatives)
- Address accuracy critical (known issues)
- Requires enterprise security (not positioned)

**Onfleet**
- Budget-conscious startups ($550+ minimum)
- Highly volatile delivery volume
- Performance-sensitive large fleets

**OptimoRoute**
- Large multi-depot operations (1k cap)
- Enterprises needing customization (limited)
- Modern UI/UX preference (outdated)

**Routific**
- Live driver tracking critical (not available)
- Feature-rich customer notifications needed
- Clustering delivery areas problematic
